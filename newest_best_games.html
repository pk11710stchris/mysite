Best Games

Kour.io

Kour.io is an action-packed shooting game that offers a variety of skins and weapons to earn. Similar to Fortnite, players engage in intense battles while progressing through ranks like gold and silver. The game is known for its strategic gameplay and vibrant community.

PUBG (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds)

PUBG is a player versus player shooter game in which up to one hundred players fight in a battle royale, a large-scale last man standing deathmatch. Players can choose to enter the match solo, duo, or with a small team of up to four people, fighting to remain the last alive.

Grand Theft Auto Online

Developed in tandem with Grand Theft Auto V, Grand Theft Auto Online was conceived as a separate experience to be played in a continually evolving world. Up to 30 players freely roam across a re-creation of the single-player world and enter lobbies to complete jobs, which are story-driven competitive and cooperative modes.

Call of Duty

Call of Duty is the world-renowned first-person shooter (FPS) franchise that has entertained players for decades. Whether you're a newcomer to the world of Call of Duty, or a long-serving veteran, there's a diversity of experiences available that cater to a variety of tastes and abilities.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox game that allows players to explore a blocky, procedurally generated 3D world, and discover and extract raw materials, craft tools and items, and build structures or earthworks. Players can either cooperate or compete in multiplayer mode, or choose survival or creative mode for their adventures.

Roblox

Roblox is an online platform and storefront where users go to play games. Roblox is not a game, but a place where people play games made by other developers. What makes Roblox different from anything else in the games industry is that all the games are made by its users.

Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular online video game developed by Epic Games. It comes in three distinct game mode versions: Fortnite: Save the World, a cooperative shooter-survival game for up to four players; Fortnite Battle Royale, a free-to-play battle royale game; and Fortnite Creative, where players can create worlds and battle arenas.

Check out these games and have fun!
