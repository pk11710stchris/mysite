Impossible Riddles

Can you solve these impossible riddles? Click on a riddle to reveal the answer!

Riddle 1: What has keys but can't open locks?

A piano.

Riddle 2: The more you take, the more you leave behind. What am I?

Footsteps.

Riddle 3: What comes once in a minute, twice in a moment, but never in a thousand years?

The letter "M".

Riddle 4: I am not alive, but I can grow. I don’t have lungs, but I need air. What am I?

Fire.

Riddle 5: What is always in front of you but can’t be seen?

The future.
